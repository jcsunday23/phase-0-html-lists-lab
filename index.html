List the ingredients for making a grilled cheese sandwich

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

ordered lists

  1. spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of the bread
  4. cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
